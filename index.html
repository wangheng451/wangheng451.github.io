About

I am a researcher at Central South University. My work lies at the intersection of large language models, transportation risk analysis, and autonomous driving.

I am currently working on LLM-driven methods for real-time traffic crash risk prediction, combining causal graph learning with large language models to improve the accuracy and interpretability of safety-critical predictions.
